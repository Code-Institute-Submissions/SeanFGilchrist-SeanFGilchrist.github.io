Beers:

Cragfast

Our own take on a West Coast IPA, Cragfast is a "West Wales" IPA with big hoppy flavours. Light in colour, refreshing and moreish. This beer was due to be a one-off April special in cask but due to the current lock-down restrictions on pubs, we have bottled it instead and so we're giving you all the chance to enjoy this one-off IPA special at home!

Cwtch

Probably the best word ever. Cwtch (rhymes with butch) can mean either cuddle or cubbyhole. Grab a glass, relax and cwtch up with this untraditional Welsh red ale - a perfect blend of six caramelly malts and three citrussy American hops.
